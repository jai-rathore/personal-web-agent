# About Jai Rathore

Jai Rathore is a Staff Software Engineer and AI Lead at Tesla, based in the San Francisco Bay Area. He has 12+ years of experience building high-impact engineering platforms from the ground up — across manufacturing, finance, HR, and now AI.

## What He's Focused On Now

Jai leads all AI initiatives across Tesla's internal factory software organization, a portfolio spanning 10+ teams and 300+ engineers. His work sits at the intersection of autonomous agents, factory operations, and engineering transformation — building systems that don't just assist engineers but actively replace manual processes entirely.

He's particularly interested in:
- **Agentic systems** – agents with real tools, memory, and autonomy rather than chatbots
- **Context engineering** – how you structure what an LLM knows determines everything
- **AI-native development** – changing how software is built, not just what it does
- **Practical AI at scale** – production agents running in real factories, not demos
